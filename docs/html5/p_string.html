pString.ptn_dollar

A pattern for string.gsub, matching a dollar sign followed by any combination of ASCII letters, numbers and underscores.

  • $FOO_bar123

pString.ptn_percent

A pattern for string.gsub, matching a two percent signs surrounding any combination of ASCII letters, numbers and underscores.

  • %FOO_bar123%
